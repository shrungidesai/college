Treatment Options for Breast Cancer

Treatment Options:

  • Surgery

    Types of surgery include lumpectomy, mastectomy, and lymph node removal.

  • Chemotherapy

    Uses drugs to destroy cancer cells throughout the body.

  • Radiation Therapy

    Uses high-energy rays to kill cancer cells.

  • Hormone Therapy

    Blocks the effects of estrogen or lowers estrogen levels to prevent cancer cell growth.

  • Targeted Therapy

    Targets specific characteristics of cancer cells to destroy them.
